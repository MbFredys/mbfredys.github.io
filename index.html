My Engineering Process

A structured approach to hardware development that minimizes risk and ensures successful project delivery.

1

Requirements & Architecture

Understanding the system, power domains, sensors, communications, and constraints.

2

Schematic Design

Creating clean and well-organized schematics with proper nets, power distribution, decoupling, and interfaces.

3

PCB Layout

Component placement and routing with focus on power integrity, signal routing, manufacturability, clearances, and design rules.

4

Review & Optimization

Identifying issues early: return paths, noise, Design for Manufacturing (DFM), component footprints, and routing problems.

5

Deliverables

Fabrication-ready Gerber files, Bill of Materials (BOM), design files, annotated report, and recommendations.
