My Engineering Process

A structured, battle-tested approach to hardware development that minimizes risk and ensures successful project delivery with manufacturing-ready results.

1

Requirements & Architecture

Understanding the system requirements, power domains, sensors, communications protocols, environmental constraints, and regulatory requirements. Creating system architecture and block diagrams.

2

Schematic Design

Creating clean, professional schematics with proper net naming, power distribution planning, decoupling capacitor placement, interface definitions, and component selection with sourcing considerations.

3

PCB Layout

Strategic component placement and precision routing with focus on power integrity, signal integrity, manufacturability, thermal management, EMI/EMC compliance, and adherence to design rules.

4

Review & Optimization

Comprehensive design validation: return path analysis, crosstalk assessment, power delivery network verification, DFM/DFA checking, thermal analysis, and manufacturability optimization.

5

Deliverables

Fabrication-ready output packages: Gerber files, ODB++, Pick & Place files, comprehensive BOM, assembly drawings, test procedures, and detailed design documentation for seamless handoff to manufacturing.
